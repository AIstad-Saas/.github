# Aistad

Aistad is a B2B SaaS platform built for Italian lawyers to manage legal fees and collect client payments — digitally, automatically, and professionally.

## What it does

A lawyer creates a structured quote with milestone-based payment schedules. An AI generates a professional Italian-language proposal. The client receives a unique link, reviews it, and accepts digitally. From there, Aistad handles everything — pro-forma issuance, Stripe payment links, overdue detection, and notifications — automatically.

No chasing clients. No manual invoicing. No missed payments.

## Stack

Next.js 14 · Supabase · Stripe Connect + Billing · Claude Haiku · Inngest · Resend · Vercel
